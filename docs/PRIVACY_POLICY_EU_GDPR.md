## 8. Your GDPR Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

### 8.1 Right to Access (Art. 15)

Request confirmation of processing and access to your data.

### 8.2 Right to Rectification (Art. 16)

Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

### 8.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten") (Art. 17)

Request deletion of your data under certain conditions.

### 8.4 Right to Restriction (Art. 18)

Limit processing of your data.

### 8.5 Right to Data Portability (Art. 20)

Receive your data in structured, machine-readable format.

### 8.6 Right to Object (Art. 21)

Object to processing based on legitimate interest.

### 8.7 Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7(3))

Withdraw consent at any time (does not affect prior processing).

### 8.8 Right to Lodge Complaint (Art. 77)

File complaint with supervisory authority:

**Data Protection Authority**  
Contact: [Your local DPA]  
Website: https://edpb.europa.eu/
