## 1. What Are Cookies

### 1.1 Definition

**Cookies** are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website or use our application. They help us:

- Remember your preferences
- Keep you logged in
- Understand how you use our platform
- Improve your experience

### 1.2 Similar Technologies

We also use:

| Technology | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| **Local Storage** | Store larger amounts of data |
| **Session Storage** | Temporary data for current session |
| **IndexedDB** | Complex data storage |
| **Web Beacons** | Track page views and email opens |
| **ETags** | Cache validation |
| **Device Fingerprinting** | Security and fraud prevention |

---

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

### 2.1 By Duration

| Type | Description | Examples |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Session Cookies** | Deleted when you close browser | Shopping cart, active session |
| **Persistent Cookies** | Remain until expiration or deletion | Remember me, preferences |

### 2.2 By Purpose

#### Strictly Necessary Cookies

**Required** for platform operation. Cannot be disabled.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `session_id` | Maintain login session | Session |
| `csrf_token` | Prevent CSRF attacks | Session |
| `auth_token` | Authentication | 30 days |

#### Functional Cookies

**Enhance** your experience with personalized features.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `language` | Remember selected language | 1 year |
| `theme` | Dark/light mode preference | 1 year |
| `notifications` | Notification preferences | 1 year |

#### Analytics Cookies

**Help us understand** how you use our platform.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics user identification | 2 years |
| `_gid` | Google Analytics session identification | 24 hours |
| `_fh_analytics` | Internal analytics | 26 months |
| `device_id` | Device identification | 1 year |

#### Marketing Cookies

**Used for** advertising and personalization (with consent).

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `_fbp` | Facebook Pixel | 90 days |
| `_gcl_au` | Google Ads conversion | 90 days |
| `ad_preferences` | Ad personalization | 1 year |

#### Third-Party Cookies

**Set by** external services we integrate.

| Service | Cookie | Purpose |
|---------|--------|---------|
| **WalletConnect** | `walletconnect` | Wallet session |
| **Supabase** | `sb-auth` | Authentication |
| **Stripe** | `__stripe_*` | Payment processing |
| **OpenAI** | N/A | API calls (no cookies) |

- Easy withdrawal of consent

**Requirements:**

## 9. Do Not Track (DNT)

### 9.1 What is DNT?

**Do Not Track** is a browser setting that signals websites not to track your browsing.

### 9.2 Our Response to DNT

We **respect** DNT signals:

- **DNT Enabled:** No analytics or marketing cookies
- **Necessary cookies:** Still applied (required for function)

### 9.3 How to Enable DNT

| Browser | Instructions |
|---------|--------------|
| **Chrome** | Settings → Privacy → Send DNT request |
| **Firefox** | Options → Privacy → Do Not Track |
| **Safari** | Preferences → Privacy → Do Not Track |
| **Edge** | Settings → Privacy → Do Not Track |
